Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need prior farming experience?

Not at all! Farmer AI is designed specifically for complete beginners. It will guide you through every step of the process, from preparing the soil to harvesting your crops.

Can the AI help me choose what to plant?

Yes! By telling the AI about your location, climate, and available space, it can recommend the best crops that are most likely to thrive in your environment.

Is Farmer AI free to use?

Yes, Farmer AI is completely free to use. Our goal is to make agricultural knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their background.

How do I get seeds and supplies?

You can use our 'Market' section to connect with trusted local farmers. They provide high-quality seeds, organic fertilizers, and other farming supplies you might need.

What if my crops get sick?

Farmer AI can help you diagnose common plant diseases and identify pests. Just describe the symptoms, and it will suggest organic and safe treatment methods.

How much land do I need to start?

You can start farming anywhere! Whether you have a large backyard, a small balcony, or just a sunny windowsill, Farmer AI will help you make the most of your available space.
